Meet your Big Sister





Hi! My name is Prabha Pelluru and I am here to be your helping hand for college admissions and job preparation! I currently work as a Financial Analyst for DUO Security (Cisco) in San Francisco, California but I am a midwestern girl! I grew up in Dublin, Ohio and graduated as Valedictorian from Dublin Jerome High School. I attended The Ohio State University on a full scholarship as a Morrill Scholarship recipient. In four years, I graduated with two degrees! I have a Finance degree from Fisher Business School and an Economics degree from the College of Arts & Sciences.

My past professional experience includes being a tutor at Kumon, home tutoring in math and english, being an Economics tutor for the Economics department at OSU and an assistant english teacher at Graham Elementary & Middle School. I’ve also interned as an Equity Research Analyst in Sydney, Australia.

In my free time, I enjoy spending time with family and friends, baking, trying new restaurants, reading and dancing!

What is Big Sister Consulting?


I am here to be your best friend to get you into your dream college and then to your dream job! I’ve personally been through the college application process and the job search so I will help you avoid the mistakes I have made and share what I have learned to become successful.

Check out my About Me page to get to know me a little. Afterwards, go through my offerings and take the first step to your future success by filling out the registration application and I will personally get in touch with you shortly after! Let's make your life easier as we do this together!
